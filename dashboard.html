Cancellation Policy

Sessions must be cancelled or rescheduled at least 48 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows will forfeit the session fee. Rescheduling is always free when done within the required window.

Payment Policy

Session fee of $300 is due at booking to reserve your date. Remaining balance for prints and products is due at the reveal appointment. We accept all major credit cards and Venmo.

Weather Policy

In the event of inclement weather, sessions will be rescheduled at no charge. Your photographer will contact you 24 hours before if weather conditions require rescheduling.
